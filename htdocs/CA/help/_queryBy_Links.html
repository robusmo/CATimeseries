National and Meteo Links

After each mouse click over the map the system will automatically give the possibility of opening an external page:

  • the National link


    • It's a webpage over Climate-Adapt portal that contains general information for the country.
    Example over France:
    http://climate-adapt.eea.europa.eu/countries/france

  • the Meteo link


    • The national website responsible for the meteo.
    Example over France:
    http://www.meteofrance.com/climat/france
