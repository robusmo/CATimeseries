Map viewer: Table of contents


All loaded datasets will added on the "Table of Contents" section, each with its individual legend:

For each layer, click on:

  • to delete the selected dataset from your list;
  • to open a webpage on the dataset's metadata;
  • to export the map as a PDF file;

Use the "drag and drop functionality" to move all datasets: remember that when the date is changed, this is always refers to the first layer (identified by a green shadow).
